History

Charlotte History

For more info or to join the league, email Charlotte@skeenation.com

The Charlotte Skeeball League, or QCskeeball, was founded at Joe’s Raw Bar in April of 2006.  Since then, the league has seen an amazing amount of growth – moving locations to Murphy’s Tavern for nearly three years and now to its current home at Blonde Lounge.  What started as 30 friends and one skeeball lane in the ultimate dive bar has grown into 4 skeeball machines and hundreds of young professionals meeting on a weekly basis to roll a few skeeballs, have a few drinks and Let The Good Times Roll!
