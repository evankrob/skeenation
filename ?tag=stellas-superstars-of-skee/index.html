Stella Artois Superstars of Week 3 (Charlotte Skeeball Skeeson X)

Tuesday, August 4th, 2009

And it’s time once again for our Stella Artois Superstars!  The winners each week receive a bucket of Stella Artois to share with skeemates and friends and toast to a hopeful continuance of their success from the previous week!

This week we honor Bert McClellan and Sara Brovet:

Bert McClellan (Skeebacca) — Bert has been in the league for a few skeesons now, but has never been able to climb out of the basement.  Even in the late night tournaments he has significant trouble finding his 40.  Well, this past Wed night, with his captain out of town, he decided to change up strategies and shoot for the hundo…and it certainly paid off as he rolled a 32.8 on the night!  By far his highest score ever!  Will Bert continue with the Hundo Experiment, or will Candyman get him back down the middle?

Sara Brovet (SpongeBob Skeepants) — Oh sweet little Sara. She spent Skeeson IX on the sidelines as scorekeeper/cheerleader for Notorious B.I.Skee.  But this summer rolled around and she was ready to break out and roll on her own, so she brought out Lori and Leslie and SpongeBob Skeepants was born.  As you can see from her scores, Sara was in a different world this past Wed and broke the 300 barrier for the first time!

Congrats to both Bert and Sara!

Stella Artois Superstars - Week 2! (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

Tuesday, July 28th, 2009

Congrats to Alice Farmer (Skee’s Company) for winning this week’s Stella Artois Superstar award!

Alice has stepped her game up this skeeson and is helping Skee’s Company climb the standings!  This is her seventh consecutive skeeson playing, and if my records are correct I don’t believe she’s ever made the High Rollers Tourney, and her teams have always missed the playoffs or crumbled in the first round. But not any more!

She rolled an astounding 337 in Week 2 and has shown that she can roll with the best of ‘em.

Congrats Alice….enjoy the Stella!

Stella’s Superstars of Skee - Week 1 (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

Monday, July 20th, 2009

Welcome to the first week of “Stella’s Superstars of Skee” brought to you by….wait for it….Stella Artois!  This will highlight a few rollers a week who have stepped their game up considerably and in doing so will receive a bucket of Stella Artois to enjoy and (hopefully) share with their skeemates.

This week’s winners?  I’m glad you asked.

Meredith Benthall (Skees of the Stone Age) - Meredith has consistently been in the basement of the skeeball rankings.  That’s not an exaggeration.  Every skeeson you can always count on Meredith for a 21 - 23 average.  Last skeeson she even won the rookskee of the year award, because after multiple skeesons, she still rolled like a rookie.  But not on Wednesday night.  Meredith rolled her career high — an astounding 29.7!  Congrats!

Steve Hilliker (K Skee Masterpiece) - Steve was an actual rookskee last skeeson, and stepped it up as the skeeson progressed, but not quite enough to qualify for the High Rollers Tourney.  He must have been practicing like crazy during the off-skeeson because he came in on Week 1 and rolled an incredible 36.1, putting him third among all rollers.  For this my friend, you deserve a Stella!  Congrats and enjoy!

PS  I love that quote — “I’d rather die of thirst than drink from the cup of mediocrity.”  I think it’s pretty obvious that he’s referring to the Champions Chalice here, and I couldn’t agree more.  Plastic cups? Metal cans? Glass Mugs? No thanks, you can keep your mediocrity.  You serve me in a Chalice or I choose to die of thirst.  That’s a strong statement and one that only a few handful of champions can say.

PPS  That Stella looks really good. I still can’t believe Blonde’s is selling it for only $3 a bottle.  They can’t be making more than ten cents a bottle off that.  It’s all about The Roosevelts baby! It’s all about The Roosevelts baby!
