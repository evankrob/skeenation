WORLD RANKINGS

The time has come.  As Charlotte and Raleigh continue to grow and Find their 40, and as Charleston and Philadelphia kick off their first skeesons, it’s time to start ranking the Skeeballers of the World in preparation for the Skee-Ball World Championships this summer!  We’re talking TV cameras, major sponsors, cash prizes, and bragging rights as “The Best Skeeballer in the World!”

The Rankings will consist of your most recent 16 weeks of skeeball action (excluding playoffs and Super Saturday) and will change week to week.  If you miss a week, that’s a zero, which will hurt your average significantly. These rankings will be used for seeding players in the first annual Skee-Ball World Championships!
