CHARLESTON

Charleston – Skeeson I

To sign a team up, follow these four easy steps:

1) Come up with a clever name

2) Find two friends to join your team

2) Email Charleston@skeenation.com with your team name, skeemates names, and email addresses

4) Let The Good Times Roll!

Email Charleston@skeenation.com with any questions or for further info.
