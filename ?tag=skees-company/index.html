Stella Artois Superstars - Week 2! (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

Tuesday, July 28th, 2009

Congrats to Alice Farmer (Skee’s Company) for winning this week’s Stella Artois Superstar award!

Alice has stepped her game up this skeeson and is helping Skee’s Company climb the standings!  This is her seventh consecutive skeeson playing, and if my records are correct I don’t believe she’s ever made the High Rollers Tourney, and her teams have always missed the playoffs or crumbled in the first round. But not any more!

She rolled an astounding 337 in Week 2 and has shown that she can roll with the best of ‘em.

Congrats Alice….enjoy the Stella!

Come and Knock on our Door… (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

Tuesday, July 28th, 2009

Alice has stepped her game up big time.  Amber found her 40 at the end of Skeeson IX and carried it over to this skeeson.  And John Ritter, well he can never be imitated or duplicated, but his new fill-in is doing a moderate job of carrying the torch.

Don’t sleep on Skee’s Company.  If Amber can stay around the 290-300 range and Alice continues to get her practice sessions in and stays in the 320+ range, expect this team to go far.  Far far far.  Currently tied with Skees of the Stone Age, they’re looking to make their move this week…
