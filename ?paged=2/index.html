Cornhole + Skeeball = Skee-Bean?

July 13th, 2009

Ever wanted to practice your skeeball skills while enjoying the warm weather outside? Well now you can…kinda.

This little bean bag toss into a ring thing is called “Skee-Bean” and can be found here for purchase.

I do have some issues with this thing though:

  1. There aren’t any hundo holes.  Why not build this as close to the real thing as possible?  10,20,30,40,50, and then two hundo holes stretched up and out on each side.
  2. The scoring is whack.  You might as well be playing Ice Ball (shudder). It looks like the scoring goes 50, 100, 500, 1000.  Yawn.
  3. Who the f*ck hired this photographer? The dude took the picture upside down.  If I were trying to sell an Easy Bake oven in a toy catalog (What??….it’s the first thing that came to mind. Don’t judge me.), I would absolutely make sure I took the picture of the product the way it was going to be used.  Certainly not upside down.
  4. This thing costs just under $70.  For some plastic rings and some bean bags.  No thanks.

All of that being said, first person to buy this thing and invite me over for a little outdoor skeeball will receive copious amounts of beer.  And a free Easy Bake Oven. Don’t ask.

Thanks to Dave “Ivan Hundo” Winans for the tip.  Raleigh is looking forward to his return in Skeeson III !

Charlotte Skeeball Goes Blonde! (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

June 30th, 2009

To quote the ever clever Michelle Kemp, “Charlotte Skeeball just got a little bit ditzy but a lot more fun!”

Truer words have never been spoken. With the unfortunate closing of Murphy’s Tavern, Charlotte Skeeball interviewed several different venues before finally deciding on Hef’s Bar and Blonde Lounge!

“We’re very excited to be hosting the league! We’ve heard a lot about this skeeball crowd, so we’re looking forward to watching them first-hand and becoming the official home of The Charlotte Skeeball League!” said Mike Heafner, owner of Hef’s and Blonde.

The league will have two chances to register for Skeeson X and to get in some Free Skee practice on Wed, July 1 and Wed, July 8 beginning at 7:00. The league will officially kick off Week 1 action on Wed, July 15th.

So get your rolling arms loosened up and c’mon out tomorrow night (wednesday, July 1st) for some Free Skee and to sign yourself up for Skeeson X ! The draft, the money ball, new venue, great specials….it’s going to be a very historic skeeson!
