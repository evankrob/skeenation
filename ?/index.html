It’s elementary, my dear Watson! (Charlotte Skeeson X)

August 4th, 2009

Kyle Watson. Wow.  A lot of you “vets” might think this guy is a rookie, but you’d be wrong.  Kyle played waaayyyyy back in the day in Skeesons II and III on Skeee….Wok! before most of you were even born.  And while he may have replaced his old skeemates with some rookies, he’s showing that he’s still got the magic touch on the skeeball lanes.  Yeah sure he hasn’t played in 7 skeesons (over 2 years), but that’s not stopping him from rolling his a$$ off.  He’s currently ranked 21st in Charlotte, with a 32.07 avg, 2 combos, and 1 Full 40.  The old “it’s like riding a bike” euphemism has never been more spot on than in this case.  Watch out for Kyle and Skeee…Wok!  Not only is he climbing the Top 32 at a rapid pace, but he’s got his squad very close to breaking into the Top 16.  Watch your back!

Stella Artois Superstars of Week 3 (Charlotte Skeeball Skeeson X)

August 4th, 2009

And it’s time once again for our Stella Artois Superstars!  The winners each week receive a bucket of Stella Artois to share with skeemates and friends and toast to a hopeful continuance of their success from the previous week!

This week we honor Bert McClellan and Sara Brovet:

Bert McClellan (Skeebacca) — Bert has been in the league for a few skeesons now, but has never been able to climb out of the basement.  Even in the late night tournaments he has significant trouble finding his 40.  Well, this past Wed night, with his captain out of town, he decided to change up strategies and shoot for the hundo…and it certainly paid off as he rolled a 32.8 on the night!  By far his highest score ever!  Will Bert continue with the Hundo Experiment, or will Candyman get him back down the middle?

Sara Brovet (SpongeBob Skeepants) — Oh sweet little Sara. She spent Skeeson IX on the sidelines as scorekeeper/cheerleader for Notorious B.I.Skee.  But this summer rolled around and she was ready to break out and roll on her own, so she brought out Lori and Leslie and SpongeBob Skeepants was born.  As you can see from her scores, Sara was in a different world this past Wed and broke the 300 barrier for the first time!

Congrats to both Bert and Sara!

Who Lives in a Pineapple Under the Skee? (Charlotte Skeeson X)

August 4th, 2009

SpongeBob Skeepants started off like any other rookie team.  Mid-700 rolls in Week 1 and 2.  But something came over them in Week 3.  Maybe it was the fact that we were running a few minutes behind on their lane which gave them extra time to envision their 40s.  Or perhaps it was the old “beer buzz vs. skeeball score” situation….maybe they had a few more than they normally would have and watched their scores skyrocket.

Whatever the reason, these rooks stepped it up big time Wed night, rolling a 925 and setting a new rookie team match record.  Keep an eye on these three beauties (note: Leslie not pictured, Jason subbed in for her last week) as the skeeson progresses, not just because they are going to continue their hot streak, but they’ve promised some crazy hot uniforms as well.

Congrats to SpongeBob Skeepants on a great Week 3!

It’s Always Sunny….in Second Place! (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

August 4th, 2009

“Those who bring sunshine to the lives of others cannot keep it from themselves.” — James Barrie

Amen Mr. Barrie, Amen.  The ladies of You are my Skee-shine just might be the nicest team in the league:  they’re super friendly with everyone, they never talk junk to their opponents, and they always have a hug to dish out or a cheer for someone rolling well.  So perhaps it’s karmic that they now find themselves in second place.

Or perhaps it’s because they’re scary good. Amethyst and Dawn were on the same squad last summer (Rockin’ Rubber Duckskees) and made it all the way to the Final Four.  And Meghan Putnam is an all-star in her own right.  If all three of these chicas can continue to find their 40 at the same time and throw up big numbers like they did Wednesday night, they may be hanging onto that #2 spot the remainder of the skeeson.

Stella Artois Superstars - Week 2! (Charlotte Skeeball League Skeeson X)

July 28th, 2009

Congrats to Alice Farmer (Skee’s Company) for winning this week’s Stella Artois Superstar award!

Alice has stepped her game up this skeeson and is helping Skee’s Company climb the standings!  This is her seventh consecutive skeeson playing, and if my records are correct I don’t believe she’s ever made the High Rollers Tourney, and her teams have always missed the playoffs or crumbled in the first round. She rolled an astounding 337 in Week 2 and has shown that she can roll with the best of ‘em.

Congrats Alice….enjoy the Stella!
