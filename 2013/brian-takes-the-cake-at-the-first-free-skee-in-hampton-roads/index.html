Brian takes the cake at the first Free Skee in Hampton Roads!

Posted in Hampton Roads Skeeball League by Farrell On February 27, 2013. No comments

There were over 30 High Rollers in attendance for the first Free Skee Tourney, and with $500 up for grabs in cash and gift cards, Brian went home as the champion, with Souvanne coming in a close second!

Come on out this Thursday night for another Free Skee tourney with the same prizes up for grabs! Millions of Beers at 8pm, Thursday 2/28! Skee there or Skee Square!
