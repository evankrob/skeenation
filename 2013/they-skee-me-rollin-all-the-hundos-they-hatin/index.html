They Skee Me Rollin’ All the Hundos, They Hatin’….

Posted in Birmingham Skeeball League by Farrell On May 15, 2013. No comments

While this squad of lovely lady rollers may currently be in the middle of the pack, you should remember that they’ve only rolled three weeks so far and anything can happen over the next few matches….especially when the league leader in Hundos is on your squad! Joy has 29 Hundos, while the next below her has 18 and then the next highest is 8. So to say she’s absolutely owning the Hundo hole and those going for it would be a severe understatement.  And now that the spotlight has been thrust upon her and her skeemates, we’ll have to see how they roll in Week 4: will they wilt in the limelight, or rise to the challenge and the pressure of being on the cover of Rolling Times magazine?
