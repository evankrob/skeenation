SkeePI Security Now Hiring: Apply Within!

Posted in Raleigh Skeeball League by Farrell On August 28, 2013. 1 Comment

Pre-season ranked in the Top 3, no one thought this team would be crumbling apart by week 4. With Evan on a 2 week international vacation, and Stone being MIA, it’s left Mikey G to scramble to find skeemates and attempt to keep his squad ranked in the upper bracket.

And so far he’s been holding his own. With the team still in 4th, that’s not a bad spot to be, especially with Evan returning soon.

Will the this team be able to pull it together in the second half of the skeeson and recover from this disastrous start and truly compete for the Chalice? Or have the other top teams already secured their team chemistry and playoff spots?
