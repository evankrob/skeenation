DeMay or DeMay Not want Charles in Charge the rest of the skeeson!

Posted in Charlotte Skeeball League by Farrell On May 7, 2013. No comments

Charles in charge of our days and our nights.
Charles in charge of our wrongs and our rights.
And I see…I want Charles in charge of me!

Well I think that about sums it up. Charles Saxon is the real life Scott Baio from Charles in Charge.  Last skeeson he was in charge of the two rookie rollers named Katie. And this skeeson now that they’ve got some experience under their belt, he’s letting them loose on the lanes a little bit. But he still remembers Chief Wahooskees glory rolls from last skeeson and aims to get the girls back to that level.  Now let’s hear the song I hope to be listening to on repeat at Sanctuary Wed night!
