Skeeson X Pre-Skeeson Rankings! (Charlotte Skeeball League)

Wednesday, July 15th, 2009

The Pre-Skeeson Rankings are a complete and total crapshoot this skeeson because there are so many variables.  There’s no telling which rookies are going to step it up and dominate and which veterans are going to choke. And don’t get me started on a little X factor called The Money Ball.  Ranking teams right now would be the equivalent of trying to pick who would win this race (pic below) from the beginning. No one could have possibly seen the fat guy tripping and falling and the guy in red (read: pink) tackling the chick as he was also going down.

Due to the all of the above-mentioned statements, there will be no pre-skeeson rankings for Skeeson X.  Even though my top three pre-skeeson pics for Skeeson IX all made it to the Final Four. But who’s counting?

Rankings or no rankings, it’s pretty obvious that I just wanted to post that picture thing up there.  I’m pretty sure it’s called a gif.  But I prefer to call it a picture thing.  Makes me laugh every time I watch it.  Kinda reminds me of those races they do at minor league games.  Folks in different color costumes racing around the bases.  Fans have to cheer for a certain color.  You know what I’m talking about, so stop acting like you don’t. If  I would have been forced to pick a color from the get go here, I would have picked the chick in black or the guy in red (read: pink).  And boy would I have been wrong.

And if I were to be forced to pick my top five teams of this skeeson, I’d go with the following:

Skeerotum - Brad Meacham, Pat Stack, and Beth Silver

Cloudy with a chance of Skeeballs - Lindsay Tutone, Jackson Patkus, and Elizabeth Mauney

You are my Skee-shine - Amethyst Klein, Meghan Putnam, and Dawn Fudge

No Team Name Yet - Kevin Ambler, Pete Walton, and Chris MacMurdo

No Team Name Yet - Brian Fudge, Josh Klein, and Bert McClellan

And BOOM there you have it.  Bring on the haters and the complainers.  Yeah yeah yeah, your team is gonna be awesome. blah blah.  I can’t help what the magic crystal skeeball tells me.  Guarantee H-Town’s team and Petot’s team are the most pissed about getting snubbed.

Skeeson X starts tonight suckas!  See ya on the lanes!
